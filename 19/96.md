<div class="title"><h1>96</h1></div>
<span class="verse thi_96_1">  <sup>1</sup>Sing to the Lord a new song; <br/>   sing to the Lord, all the earth. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_2">  <sup>2</sup>Sing to the Lord, praise his name; <br/>   proclaim his salvation day after day. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_3">  <sup>3</sup>Declare his glory among the nations, <br/>   his marvelous deeds among all peoples. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_4">  <sup>4</sup>For great is the Lord and most worthy of praise; <br/>   he is to be feared above all gods. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_5">  <sup>5</sup>For all the gods of the nations are idols, <br/>   but the Lord made the heavens. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_6">  <sup>6</sup>Splendor and majesty are before him; <br/>   strength and glory are in his sanctuary. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_7">  <sup>7</sup>Ascribe to the Lord, all you families of nations, <br/>   ascribe to the Lord glory and strength. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_8">  <sup>8</sup>Ascribe to the Lord the glory due his name; <br/>   bring an offering and come into his courts. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_9">  <sup>9</sup>Worship the Lord in the splendor of his<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Or Lordwith the splendor of">⚓</a> holiness; <br/>   tremble before him, all the earth. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_10">  <sup>10</sup>Say among the nations, “The Lord reigns.” <br/>   The world is firmly established, it cannot be moved; <br/>   he will judge the peoples with equity. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_11">  <sup>11</sup>Let the heavens rejoice, let the earth be glad; <br/>   let the sea resound, and all that is in it. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_12">  <sup>12</sup>Let the fields be jubilant, and everything in them; <br/>   let all the trees of the forest sing for joy. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_96_13">  <sup>13</sup>Let all creation rejoice before the Lord, for he comes, <br/>   he comes to judge the earth. <br/>  He will judge the world in righteousness <br/>   and the peoples in his faithfulness. <br/></span>
