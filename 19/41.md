<div class="title"><h1>41</h1><i>For the director of music. A psalm of David.</i></div>
<span class="verse thi_41_1">  <sup>1</sup>Blessed are those who have regard for the weak; <br/>   the Lord delivers them in times of trouble. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_2">  <sup>2</sup>The Lord protects and preserves them— <br/>   they are counted among the blessed in the land— <br/>   he does not give them over to the desire of their foes. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_3">  <sup>3</sup>The Lord sustains them on their sickbed <br/>   and restores them from their bed of illness. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_4">  <sup>4</sup>I said, “Have mercy on me, Lord; <br/>   heal me, for I have sinned against you.” <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_5">  <sup>5</sup>My enemies say of me in malice, <br/>   “When will he die and his name perish?” <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_6">  <sup>6</sup>When one of them comes to see me, <br/>   he speaks falsely, while his heart gathers slander; <br/>   then he goes out and spreads it around. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_7">  <sup>7</sup>All my enemies whisper together against me; <br/>   they imagine the worst for me, saying, <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_8">  <sup>8</sup>“A vile disease has afflicted him; <br/>   he will never get up from the place where he lies.” <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_9">  <sup>9</sup>Even my close friend, <br/>   someone I trusted, <br/>  one who shared my bread, <br/>   has turned<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Hebrew has lifted up his heel">⚓</a> against me. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_10">  <sup>10</sup>But may you have mercy on me, Lord; <br/>   raise me up, that I may repay them. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_11">  <sup>11</sup>I know that you are pleased with me, <br/>   for my enemy does not triumph over me. <br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_12">  <sup>12</sup>Because of my integrity you uphold me <br/>   and set me in your presence forever. <br/><br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_41_13">  <sup>13</sup>Praise be to the Lord, the God of Israel, <br/>   from everlasting to everlasting. <br/> <aside style="text-align:center;">Amen and Amen. </aside><br/></span>
